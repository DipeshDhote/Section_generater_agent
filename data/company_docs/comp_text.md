# Bharat Tech Solutions Private Limited

## Company Overview

Bharat Tech Solutions Private Limited is a leading Indian IT infrastructure and digital transformation company established in 2018. The organization provides end-to-end technology solutions for government departments, public sector undertakings (PSUs), and private enterprises across India.

The company focuses on enabling secure, scalable, and future-ready IT systems aligned with the Government of India’s Digital India initiative.

## Core Capabilities & Services

- Installation and maintenance of IP-based CCTV surveillance systems for smart city and security projects
